Design your Network: Construction Elements

These simple construction elements can help you make flyers and diagrams for your network. If you're on a computer, you can copy and paste the elements into your workspace. The elements also work fine with scissors and glue.

External Resources

If you want to make a diagram with these Elements on a computer, you can find free, open source graphic design programs available online. Look for Inkscape for drawing diagrams and Gimp for working with photos. Google Docs also has a free drawing tool.
